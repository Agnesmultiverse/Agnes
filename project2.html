Grow Up or Not

Agnesverse Log: “Grow Up or Not”

In this universe, Alice dreams of adulthood—a world she imagines as exciting and full of freedom. But when she steps into it, she’s overwhelmed. Lost in a crowd of towering adults, drowning in piles of work, and crushed by endless responsibilities, Alice realizes growing up isn’t the escape she hoped for. This story reflects the pressure and competition society forces on young people today, leaving them exhausted before they even begin.

Agnes’ Multiverse Manifesto: “Grow Up or Not”

Alice’s story is a reflection of the intense pressure young people face to grow up, compete, and succeed in a world that demands too much too soon. We’re told adulthood is the goal, but in reality, it often feels like a race where joy is sacrificed for survival. Through Alice, I want to challenge the notion that success means exhaustion and call out the relentless pressure to keep up. Growing up shouldn’t mean losing ourselves in the process.
