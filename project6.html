Agnesverse Log: “Agnesverse Log”

Welcome to another corner of the Agnesverse, where Agnes—this time with armpit hair, crooked teeth, and wrinkles—is about to challenge the status quo. She strolls into a hair salon, feeling fabulous, only to have the stylist gush over her “sexy lips.” Delighted, Agnes grins, flashing her crooked teeth. But the stylist freaks out, dialing 911 to haul her off to Bad Teeth Prison.

Once inside, Agnes is greeted by other “bad teeth” inmates, but when they spot her wrinkles, they decide they’re “too ugly” and slap a mask on her. Agnes reaches her breaking point. With a bold raise of her arms, she reveals her proud armpit hair, sending everyone scrambling in horror. Agnes is left standing strong, fully herself, in a world too afraid to look.

Agnes’ Multiverse Manifesto: “Agnesverse Log”

This chapter of Agnes’ multiverse unravels the absurd and stifling rules society places on women’s bodies. Agnes, in all her hairy, wrinkled, toothy glory, faces not just the judgment of men, but also the internalized gaze of women conditioned to enforce these standards. Here, the Agnesverse asks, why are we so afraid of what doesn’t fit? This universe is a playground of societal absurdities—where women’s “flaws” become crimes, and our imperfections get locked up.
